Nike.com Product Detail Page

Driving 108% revenue growth through platform optimization and global coordination

Situation

Context: Nike.com's Product Detail Page (PDP) was the core e-commerce experience where customers made purchase decisions. The platform was running on legacy infrastructure that struggled during peak traffic events (holiday shopping like Black Friday in the U.S. and Singles Day in China, major product launches).

Business Need: Drive revenue growth in digital channels while ensuring platform stability and consistency across global markets (North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific).

Challenge: The platform needed to support increasing traffic volumes, regional customization requirements, and new features without sacrificing performance or user experience.

Task

My Responsibility: As Senior PM for Nike.com PDP, I owned the product vision, roadmap, and execution for two major platform refactors that would modernize the infrastructure and optimize the user experience.

Key Goals:

  • Increase revenue through conversion rate optimization
  • Improve platform stability and performance during peak traffic
  • Support regional requirements (legal, currency, localization)
  • Enable rapid feature iteration
  • Maintain seamless user experience during migration

Action

Phase 1: Data-Driven Discovery

  • Analyzed conversion data, bounce rates, and user flows to identify bottlenecks
  • Identified that page load time correlated directly with conversion drop-off
  • Mapped regional requirements (EU data privacy, China fulfillment, APAC pricing)

Phase 2: Strategic Planning

  • Defined MVP for cloud migration (infrastructure modernization without UX overhaul)
  • Created roadmap to prioritize: stability → performance → new features
  • Negotiated with stakeholders to balance speed with regional compliance

Phase 3: Execution & Collaboration

  • Led cross-functional team of backend engineers, frontend developers, and QA
  • Consulted with geographic partners (EU legal, China ops, APAC marketing) to clarify requirements
  • Managed dependencies and milestones to ensure phased rollout
  • Established clear requirements and acceptance criteria for engineering teams

Phase 4: Optimization & Scaling

  • A/B tested design changes to maximize conversion
  • Monitored performance metrics during rollout
  • Deployed 'Nike By You' customizable footwear experience on new platform
  • Collected data to drive next iteration of improvements
Key Decision: We chose cloud migration over a complete redesign. This allowed us to modernize infrastructure without the risk of a UI overhaul. Once stable, we could iterate on design with confidence.

Result

Revenue Impact: 108% revenue growth over 4 years for Nike Digital, driven significantly by platform improvements and feature enhancements.

Technical Outcomes:

  • Successfully migrated PDP to cloud platform
  • Reduced page load time by 30%+
  • Improved uptime during peak traffic events
  • Enabled support for 40+ regional variants

Business Outcomes:

  • Conversion rate improved by 15%
  • Average order value increased through better product recommendations
  • Foundation set for Nike By You 3D customization at scale

Learned & Impact

Key Insight: Platform stability isn't just a technical concern—it's a product strategy decision. By investing in infrastructure first, we created runway for innovation.

Why This Matters for Hiring Managers:

  • I understand that technical constraints shape product roadmaps
  • I can manage complex stakeholder needs across geographies
  • I prioritize customer impact over feature velocity
  • I communicate trade-offs clearly and transparently
  • I translate business goals into technical requirements

Application to Your Role: At a company like HALO working with enterprise clients, managing regional requirements and balancing platform stability with customization is exactly the type of challenge I thrive in.

Nike By You 3D Customization

Solving technical bottlenecks at unprecedented scale through data-driven problem solving

Situation

Context: Nike By You is a 3D customization experience where customers design their own footwear. The technology renders 2D design assets in real-time as users make customization choices.

Business Opportunity: 3D customization was a major differentiator. Nike wanted to roll out the feature globally to drive engagement and justify premium pricing.

Technical Challenge: The rendering infrastructure couldn't scale. During high-traffic events (holiday shopping, major product launches), the system hit a hard ceiling of 400 renders per second—far below what global adoption would require.

Deadline Pressure: Nike had committed to rolling out 3D customization to 40-50% of products within 12 months.

Task

My Responsibility: Lead the back-end services team to identify and solve the rendering bottleneck, enabling global scale.

Constraints:

  • Timeline: 12 months to ship at scale
  • Technical: Unknown architecture limitations
  • Business: Couldn't delay product launch; brand impact was high

Action

Phase 1: Data-Driven Problem Identification

  • Reviewed infrastructure metrics and identified rendering service as the bottleneck
  • Analyzed traffic patterns during high-volume events
  • Determined: 400 RPS limit would prevent global adoption
  • Calculated required capacity: near-infinite RPS to handle peak traffic

Phase 2: Cross-Functional Collaboration

  • Worked with backend architects to understand rendering pipeline
  • Partnered with infrastructure/DevOps to evaluate scaling options
  • Collaborated with product design to understand real-time rendering requirements

Phase 3: Solution Architecture

  • Led requirements gathering to define what 'infinite scale' meant
  • Prioritized: User experience (750ms render time target) over perfect visual fidelity
  • Created roadmap to re-architect rendering services in phases

Phase 4: Execution & Optimization

  • Optimized 2D asset rendering for 750ms load time (from 2+ seconds)
  • Implemented caching and parallel processing to achieve near-infinite RPS
  • Tested with realistic traffic patterns from previous peak events
  • Rolled out gradually, monitoring for issues
Key Decision: Rather than trying to perfect the visual rendering, we optimized for user experience. 750ms render time is "fast enough" for users, but breakthrough from a technical perspective.

Result

Technical Achievement:

  • Scaled rendering infrastructure from 400 RPS to near-infinite RPS
  • Reduced render time from 2+ seconds to 750ms
  • "No one in the entire world is doing anything like this at this magnitude of scale"

Business Impact:

  • Successfully rolled out 3D customization to 40-50% of Nike.com and Nike App products
  • Enabled seamless experience during holiday shopping and major launches
  • Created competitive moat: unique capability that competitors can't easily replicate
  • Generated significant premium revenue from customized products

Operations Impact:

  • Saved Nike's Operations team 1,040 hours annually through workflow optimization

Learned & Impact

Key Insight: Technical problems are often product problems. By partnering closely with engineers and using data to define requirements, we solved an infrastructure challenge that enabled a business opportunity.

Why This Matters for Hiring Managers:

  • I'm comfortable working on complex technical challenges
  • I use data to identify and prioritize problems
  • I understand how technical constraints drive product decisions
  • I can translate between technical and business stakeholders
  • I balance perfection with pragmatism

Application to Your Role: If you're working with enterprise clients who have complex technical requirements (API integrations, data migration, compliance), this experience shows I can navigate those conversations with confidence.

Finance Transformation (Anaplan Integration)

Unifying global financial planning across 3+ regions with complex stakeholder management

Situation

Context: Nike's Finance organization was using fragmented planning tools across regions (North America, Greater China, Europe). This led to forecasting inconsistencies, slow planning cycles, and inability to do real-time financial decision-making.

Strategic Goal: Unify financial planning on a single platform (Anaplan) to improve forecasting accuracy, accelerate planning cycles, and enable better business decisions.

Complexity: Each region had different requirements, constraints, and stakeholders:

  • North America: Fastest cycle, most complex products
  • Greater China: Strict data residency and regulatory requirements
  • Europe: GDPR compliance, multi-currency complexity

Task

My Responsibility (as Senior Principal Consultant/TPM): Lead technical product management for the finance transformation initiative. Translate business requirements into product capabilities while managing cross-regional dependencies and ensuring delivery.

Key Challenges:

  • Multiple stakeholders with competing priorities
  • Technical complexity (Anaplan configuration, data architecture)
  • Regulatory constraints (China data residency, GDPR)
  • Time zones (synchronous communication across 3 continents)

Action

Phase 1: Customer-Centric Discovery

  • Conducted deep discovery with Finance teams in each region
  • Mapped planning workflows and identified pain points
  • Understood regional constraints (regulatory, operational, technical)
  • Built detailed requirements for Anaplan configuration

Phase 2: Roadmap Prioritization

  • Identified common platform capabilities (foundation)
  • Planned regional customizations (phase 2 rollouts)
  • Prioritized by business value and dependency
  • Balanced speed with regional complexity

Phase 3: Cross-Regional Collaboration

  • Facilitated weekly syncs with Finance, Engineering, Data, and regional teams
  • Managed time zone challenges (overlapping meetings, async documentation)
  • Negotiated trade-offs when regions had conflicting needs
  • Ensured alignment on scope, timelines, and success metrics

Phase 4: Regulatory & Compliance

  • Navigated China data residency requirements (data can't leave China)
  • Ensured GDPR compliance for European data handling
  • Coordinated security compliance migrations
  • Documented requirements for each region's legal constraints

Phase 5: Execution & Communication

  • Analyzed detailed Anaplan design documents to extract requirements
  • Created product roadmap documents for leadership review
  • Provided weekly health status updates to stakeholders
  • Tracked delivery against milestones
Key Decision: Rather than force all regions into identical processes, we built a flexible platform where regions could customize within guardrails. This allowed faster time-to-value while maintaining data consistency.

Result

Platform Delivery:

  • Successfully launched unified Anaplan platform across North America, Greater China, and Europe
  • Navigated complex data migration for China (regulatory compliance)
  • Implemented regional customizations without compromising data integrity

Business Impact:

  • Reduced planning cycle time by 30%+
  • Improved forecast accuracy through consistent methodology
  • Enabled real-time financial decision-making
  • Created single source of truth for global planning

Operational Outcomes:

  • Improved cross-regional collaboration and communication
  • Established scalable platform for future growth
  • Enhanced compliance and audit capabilities

Learned & Impact

Key Insight: Enterprise product management is about managing complexity, not just building features. The real skill is aligning diverse stakeholders, managing constraints, and delivering value despite complexity.

Why This Matters for Hiring Managers:

  • I understand enterprise complexity (multiple regions, regulatory constraints, competing priorities)
  • I can manage stakeholder relationships without formal authority
  • I communicate clearly to both technical and business audiences
  • I prioritize customer value over internal politics
  • I'm comfortable with ambiguity and constraints

Application to Your Role: HALO works with large enterprise clients like CVS and PepsiCo through RFPs. This experience demonstrates I can understand complex business requirements, navigate stakeholder dynamics, and deliver solutions that balance speed with quality.
